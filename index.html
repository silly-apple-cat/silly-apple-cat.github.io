What is EDM?

Electronic Dance Music (EDM) is a genre of music, which typically has a repetitive beat and uses electronic instruments.

It is often seen as being upbeat and energetic, which fits into the context of Geometry Dash.

What is Geometry Dash?

Geometry Dash is a music platformer game developed by Robert Topola. In Geometry Dash, the player must control the icon on the screen, avoiding obstacles to reach the end of a level.

Users can create their own levels, and these levels can use songs published on https://www.newgrounds.com/, and also the large collection of songs in the in-game library.

How are these interlinked?

Geometry Dash levels are often flashy, fast-paced and futuristic, and the genre of EDM fits perfectly with this theme.

Because of this, many Geometry Dash levels have utilised music from the EDM genre.
