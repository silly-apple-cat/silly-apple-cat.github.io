What is this website?

I created this website to showcase EDM music, specifically in the context of Geometry Dash.

Why Geometry Dash?

Geometry Dash is my favourite game, and I love the music from the game.

Why EDM?

Geometry Dash uses lots of music from the EDM genre, and therefore it made sense for me to create a website based on both.
