Introduction

Throughout the history of Geometry Dash, a plethora of songs have been used by creators. Artists such as Creo, Bossfight, F-777 and more, have created music which is used in the game.

Geometry Dash has been heavily influenced by the EDM genre, but there are also other genres of music which are used in the game, such as Pop.
